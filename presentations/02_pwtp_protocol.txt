PWTP: THE FUTURE OF 3D STREAMING
===SLIDE===
THE PROBLEM: BANDWIDTH CRISIS
- 80% of internet traffic is video
- 4K/8K streaming is unsustainable
- 3D content requires 100x more data
- Global infrastructure cannot scale
- Rural and developing areas left behind
===SLIDE===
THE SOLUTION: POLYNOMIAL COEFFICIENTS
- Replace pixels with polynomial functions
- Store coefficients, not bitmaps
- Reconstruct from math, not buffers
- One coefficient set = infinite resolution
- No quality loss at any zoom level
===SLIDE===
THE WALK EXAMPLE
- 15-minute Pompeii walkthrough: 600KB
- Equivalent MP4: 420MB
- Compression ratio: 700,000:1
- Streams on 2G networks
- Loads in under 2 seconds
