1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

PolyArt Technologies Ltd proposes the deployment of a therapeutic polynomial
art platform across 20 NHS trusts and mental health organisations. The
platform provides evidence-based art therapy modules for anxiety, depression,
PTSD, and dementia, with integrated patient progress tracking, clinician
training for 500 therapists, clinical trials support for three studies,
research publication partnerships, patient home access licenses, and full
integration with NHS digital infrastructure. The total investment of
GBP 1,000,000 covers 18 months of deployment and 12 months of clinical
trials. This programme is projected to deliver a return on investment of
400% through reduced treatment costs, improved patient outcomes, and NICE
accreditation.

================================================================================
2. SCOPE OF ENGAGEMENT
================================================================================

This proposal encompasses the end-to-end deployment of a clinically validated
digital therapeutics platform across the NHS, combining polynomial art
creation with established art therapy methodologies.

  Therapeutic Platform for 20 NHS Trusts:
    - Cloud-hosted platform (NHS Data Security and Protection Toolkit compliant)
    - Integration with NHS Spine and clinical systems (HL7 FHIR)
    - Hosted on NHS-approved cloud infrastructure (UK data residency)
    - 20 trust deployments across England (mental health and acute trusts)
    - Concurrent capacity: 2,000 patients and 500 clinicians

  Art Therapy Modules:
    - Anxiety Module: polynomial colour therapy, guided breathing visualisation,
     渐进式 pattern complexity, session-by-session progression
    - Depression Module: mood-responsive polynomial generation, positive
      reinforcement algorithms, social sharing (opt-in), achievement tracking
    - PTSD Module: trauma-informed design, controlled exposure through
      polynomial abstraction, therapist-controlled parameters, safety protocols
    - Dementia Module: memory-triggering polynomial patterns, familiar colour
      palettes, simplified interface, carer-assisted mode, cognitive
      stimulation exercises
    - Each module: 12 structured sessions, 45-60 minutes per session
    - Evidence-based design informed by NICE guidelines and clinical literature

  Patient Progress Tracking:
    - Standardised outcome measures (PHQ-9, GAD-7, PCL-5, MMSE)
    - Automated scoring and trend visualisation
    - Clinician dashboard with real-time patient monitoring
    - Alert system for deterioration or non-engagement
    - Monthly outcome reports for trust management

  Clinician Training Programme:
    - 500 therapists trained across 20 trusts
    - Accredited training programme (CPD points: 20 per therapist)
    - In-person workshops (3 days per cohort, 10 cohorts)
    - Online learning management system with certification
    - Ongoing clinical supervision support (quarterly webinars)

  Clinical Trials Support:
    - 3 clinical trials designed and supported
    - Trial 1: Randomised Controlled Trial (RCT) for anxiety (n=200)
    - Trial 2: Feasibility study for depression (n=100)
    - Trial 3: Pilot study for dementia (n=60)
    - Statistical analysis support
    - Ethical approval facilitation
    - Patient recruitment materials and processes

  Research Publication Partnership:
    - Joint publications in peer-reviewed journals
    - Target: 6 publications over 24 months
    - Open-access publication fees covered
    - Conference presentations (target: 4 conferences)
    - Plain English summaries for patients and public

  Patient Home Access Licences:
    - 5,000 home access licences for patient use
    - Tablet lending programme (500 devices for patients without access)
    - Carer portal for family involvement (opt-in)
    - Telehealth integration for remote therapy sessions
    - 24/7 technical support helpline for patients

  NHS Digital Infrastructure Integration:
    - NHS Smart Card authentication
    - Integration with Electronic Patient Records (EPR)
    - NHS App compatible patient interface
    - Data shared via NHS Spine (HL7 FHIR R4)
    - Cyber Essentials Plus and ISO 27001 compliant
    - NHS Data Security and Protection Toolkit (DSPT) certified
