1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

PolyArt Technologies Ltd proposes a major summer exhibition at the Royal
Academy of Arts titled "Mathematics as Art: The Polynomial Collection."
The exhibition will showcase 50 commissioned polynomial artworks, an
interactive digital wing, a published catalogue (10,000 copies), educational
outreach to 100 schools, international touring potential across three
venues, a comprehensive press and media campaign, and a VIP opening event.
The total investment of GBP 1,000,000 covers nine months of preparation
and a three-month exhibition period. This programme is projected to deliver
a return on investment of 250% through ticket sales, catalogue sales, and
international touring fees.

================================================================================
2. SCOPE OF ENGAGEMENT
================================================================================

This proposal encompasses the conception, curation, and delivery of a
flagship summer exhibition at the Royal Academy of Arts, Burlington House,
London W1.

  Exhibition Content:
    - 50 commissioned polynomial artworks (physical prints and digital displays)
    - Works by 20 artists (emerging and established, UK and international)
    - Thematic sections: Symmetry, Fractals, Topology, Chaos, Harmony
    - Interactive digital wing: 5 real-time generative art stations
    - Immersive projection room (8m x 8m, 360-degree polynomial visuals)

  Catalogue Publication:
    - Hardback catalogue: "Mathematics as Art: The Polynomial Collection"
    - Print run: 10,000 copies
    - Essays by 5 academic contributors (mathematics and art history)
    - Full-colour plates of all 50 artworks
    - ISBN registered, distributed via Royal Academy shop and online
    - Digital edition available via RA website

  Educational Outreach:
    - School visit programme: 100 schools (Key Stages 2-5)
    - Capacity: 15,000 students over exhibition period
    - Teacher preview mornings (2 sessions)
    - Workshop materials and lesson plans provided
    - Student competition: "Design Your Polynomial" (national)

  International Touring:
    - Touring package prepared for 3 international venues
    - Target venues: Centre Pompidou (Paris), Museum of Modern Art (New York),
      Museum of Contemporary Art (Tokyo)
    - Modular exhibition design for venue adaptation
    - Touring logistics and insurance managed by PolyArt

  Press and Media Campaign:
    - National press launch (target: broadsheet coverage in 10+ titles)
    - Broadcast media partnerships (BBC Arts, Sky Arts)
    - Social media campaign (target: 3 million impressions)
    - Influencer programme (20 art and mathematics commentators)
    - Review copies distributed to 50+ critics

  VIP Opening Event:
    - Gala evening for 500 guests (patrons, sponsors, media, artists)
    - Keynote address by a prominent mathematician or artist
    - Champagne reception and private viewing
    - Commemorative exhibition poster for all attendees
    - Live polynomial art demonstration
