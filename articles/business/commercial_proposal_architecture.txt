Executive Summary
-----------------
PolyArt Architectural Design Suite is a comprehensive parametric design platform
that integrates polynomial surface modeling, generative design optimization, and
sustainability analysis into a unified workflow. This proposal covers licensing,
training, BIM integration, and ongoing support for architectural firms and
construction companies seeking to differentiate their practice through
mathematically-driven design.

The Design Challenge
--------------------
Contemporary architecture demands the simultaneous optimization of aesthetics,
structural performance, energy efficiency, and sustainability. Traditional design
tools require architects to evaluate these criteria separately, often resulting
in compromises that sacrifice one dimension for another. Polynomial-based
parametric design enables simultaneous multi-criteria optimization, producing
buildings that excel across all performance dimensions. Firms that adopt these
tools gain a measurable competitive advantage in design quality, client
satisfaction, and project efficiency.
