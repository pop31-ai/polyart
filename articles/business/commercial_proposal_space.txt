Executive Summary
-----------------
PolyArt Space Visualization is a comprehensive platform for planetary surface
modeling, mission planning visualization, astronaut training, and public outreach
in space exploration programs. This proposal covers platform development,
integration with mission planning workflows, and training programs tailored to
the needs of government space agencies and commercial space companies.

The Mission Need
----------------
Space exploration generates data of extraordinary complexity and volume. Mission
planners must visualize orbital mechanics, planetary terrain, spacecraft
trajectories, and environmental conditions simultaneously. Astronauts require
realistic training environments that accurately represent the destinations they
will visit. Public outreach demands compelling visual narratives that inspire
support and funding. PolyArt Space Visualization addresses all three needs
through polynomial surface modeling and real-time rendering technology.

Platform Capabilities
---------------------
  - Polynomial surface reconstruction from planetary probe data
  - Real-time orbital trajectory visualization
  - Spacecraft design and aerodynamic modeling
  - Astronaut training environment generation
  - Public outreach and marketing content creation
  - Mission control display integration
  - Multi-user collaborative planning environment

Deliverables

Mission Planning Visualization:
  - Orbital mechanics simulation
  - Trajectory optimization and visualization

Astronaut Training:

Public Outreach:
