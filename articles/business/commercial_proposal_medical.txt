Executive Summary
-----------------
PolyArt Medical Visualization is an FDA-track medical imaging platform that
transforms diagnostic data into intuitive polynomial surface representations.
This proposal covers platform licensing, clinical integration, training, and
regulatory support for hospital networks and medical schools seeking to improve
diagnostic accuracy, surgical planning, and medical education through advanced
visualization technology.

The Clinical Need
-----------------
Modern medical imaging produces data of extraordinary volume and complexity.
Physicians must mentally reconstruct three-dimensional anatomy from hundreds
of two-dimensional slices, a cognitive task that introduces interpretation
variability and diagnostic error. Studies show that polynomial surface
reconstruction of CT and MRI data improves spatial understanding of complex
anatomy by forty percent compared to conventional slice-by-slice review.
The PolyArt Medical platform addresses this need by providing real-time
polynomial surface rendering that preserves anatomical detail while enhancing
visual clarity.

Platform Capabilities
