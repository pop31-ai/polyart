1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

PolyArt Technologies Ltd proposes the design, construction, and operation
of a permanent interactive installation within the Turbine Hall at Tate
Modern. The installation, titled "Polynomial Perspectives," will comprise
three polynomial art chambers (each 10m x 6m) featuring real-time visitor
interaction via touch and motion sensors. The programme includes seasonal
content rotation four times per year, an educational programme reaching
50,000 students annually, a marketing partnership leveraging Tate branding,
and comprehensive maintenance and technical support over three years. The
total investment of GBP 1,000,000 is projected to deliver a return on
investment of 280% through increased ticket sales, merchandise revenue,
and enhanced brand value.

================================================================================
2. SCOPE OF ENGAGEMENT
================================================================================

This proposal encompasses the end-to-end delivery of a permanent
interactive art installation at Tate Modern, comprising:

  Installation Design and Construction:
    - 3 polynomial art chambers (each 10m x 6m, 3m ceiling height)
    - High-resolution LED wall panels (8K resolution per chamber)
    - Real-time polynomial rendering engine (60fps, sub-10ms latency)
    - Touch-sensitive surfaces (capacitive, 40-point multi-touch)
    - Motion sensor arrays (LiDAR, 12 sensors per chamber)
    - Ambient spatial audio systems (Dolby Atmos configuration)

  Content and Interaction:
    - 12 core polynomial art pieces (4 per chamber)
    - Visitor-driven generative art: touch/motion alters polynomial parameters
    - Real-time visualisation of mathematical transformations
    - Accessibility features: audio descriptions, tactile interfaces,
      wheelchair-accessible interaction zones
    - Seasonal content rotation: 4 new pieces per year (12 total over 3 years)

  Educational Programme:
    - Curriculum-linked workshops for Key Stages 2-4
    - Capacity: 50,000 students per year (1,000 school visits annually)
    - Teacher resource packs aligned with National Curriculum
    - Dedicated education coordinator (full-time, 3-year contract)
    - Online learning platform with pre/post-visit materials

  Marketing Partnership:
    - Co-branded marketing materials (Tate and PolyArt logos)
    - Joint press releases for installation launch and seasonal rotations
    - Social media campaign support (target: 5 million impressions Year 1)
    - Feature in Tate Members magazine (quarterly, 600,000 circulation)
    - Inclusion in Tate annual report and exhibition programme

  Maintenance and Technical Support:
    - 24/7 on-call technical support
    - Preventive maintenance schedule (weekly inspections)
    - Hardware refresh cycle: Year 2 component upgrade
    - Dedicated on-site technician during opening hours
    - Remote monitoring dashboard with real-time diagnostics
