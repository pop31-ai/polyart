Zhaiyao
-------
Benwen tantao le duoxiangshi shuxue yu zhiwu yishu de jiaoji, zhanshi
canshuqu he jizuo quxian ruhe yimo ziran huahui xingtai, qiejindu
lingren jingtan. Women tichu PolyArt zhiwu moni kuangjia, yong
FlowerCurves, PlantCurves he CompositionCurves deng lei lai
shengcheng huahui moshi. Tongguo yanjiu huangjinjiao, yexu xulie he
Turing moshi deng shuxue yuanli, women jieshi le suanfa ruhe bianma
ziran zhong de mei. Women hai taolun le huashu, huayuan changjing,
huahuan he ikebana deng zuhe jishu.

Guanjianci: duoxiangshi quxian, zhiwu jianmo, shengchengshi yishu,
jizuo zuobiao, yexu xulie, suanfa mei.


1. Shuxue huahui moshi
-----------------------
Zui jiandan de zhiwu quxian shi meiguihua, yong jizuo zuobiao
dingyi:

    r = a * (1 + cos(k * theta))

qizhong 'a' kongzhi daxiao, 'k' jueding huaban shuliang. Dang k
shi zhengzhengshu shi, meiguihua chansheng zhenghao k pian huaban
(huo zhe 2k, ru guo k shi oushu). Gaibian k keyi huo de类si
xiangri kui, maodan he yinhua de xingtai.

Baihehua juyou liuzhou duicheng, keyi tongguo xiugai meiguihua
fangcheng yong jiaohe xiebo jianmo:

    r = a * (1 + cos(3 * theta)) * (1 + 0.3 * cos(6 * theta))

zhe hui chansheng liu pian mingque de huaban, youxì de neibu jiegou,
yulei shi Lilium shu.

Xiangrikuizhong de jihua shun Fibonacci shulie. Zhongzi de
pailie shun:

    theta_n = n * phi,  r_n = c * sqrt(n)

qizhong phi = (1 + sqrt(5)) / 2 shi huangjinbizhi (~1.618), c shi
suo fang changshu. Mei ge houxian zhongzi rao huangjinjiao
(~137.508 du) xuanzhuan, xingcheng xiangrikuizhong de jingdian
luoxuanhuawen.

Qita huaban quxian keyi yong leishisi de canshu fangshi dingyi:

    x(t) = R * cos(t) * (1 + epsilon * cos(m * t))
    y(t) = R * sin(t) * (1 + epsilon * cos(m * t))

qizhong m jueding bo lang shu, epsilon tiaozhi shendu.


2. PolyArt zhiwu moni kuangjia
-------------------------------
PolyArt kuangjia jiang zhiwu quxian shengcheng zuzhi cheng san ge
heixin lei:

  FlowerCurves: Shengcheng geren de huatou. Fangfa bao kuo
  rose(a, k), lily(a, n), daisy(center, radius, nPetals) he
  sunflower(nSeeds, scale). Mei ge fangfa fanhui yi zu jizuo huo
  canshu quxian dian, keyi zhijie huizhi.

  PlantCurves: Moni jing, ye he fenjhi jiegou. Shiyong digui L
  xitong guize peihe duoxiangshi chajian. Zhuyao fangfa:
  stem(start, end, curvature), leaf(base, angle, size),
  branch(point, depth, spread).

  CompositionCurves: Chuli duoge zhiwu yuanke de kongjian fenbu.
  Zhichi fangshe fenbu, wangge buzhi he zidingyi fangzhi.
  Fangfa: bouquet(flowers, center, radius), garden(layout, density),
  wreath(elements, radius, count).

Suo you lei gongxiang tongyi de jiekou, yongyu yanse yingse, xiankuang
he bianhuan (pingyi, xuanzhuan, suofang). Gai moni kuangjia yong
Python shixian, kexuan yong NumPy jiasu lai piliang chuli dazhongzi
shuju.

FlowerCurves lei hai zhichi hunhe xingtai, tongguo xianxing
chajian hunhe liang tiao jichu quxian:

    r_hunhe = (1 - t) * r_huahui1 + t * r_huaahui2

zhe keyi chuangzao guodu xing zhiwu xingtai, ronghe liangge
butong wuzhong de tedian.


3. Suanfa mei
-------------
Ziran de mei zunxun shuxue guize. Huangjinjiao (137.508 du)
kongzhi yexu xulie -- ye, zhongzi he huaban de pailie. Zhe ge jiao
tui dao wei:

    alpha = 360 / phi^2 = 360 * (2 - phi) = 360 * (3 - sqrt(5)) / 2

meiyou renhe youlishu neng hao de jinni zhe ge jiao, zhe baozheng
linjin de liang ge yuanke buhui chongdie, zuida hua guangzhao he
chuanbozhe de jiechu.

Alan Turing tichu fanying-kuisan xitong chansheng ziran huawen.
Zai huaban shang, liang ge huaxue morphogen huxiang zuoyong:

    du/dt = D_u * grad^2(u) - u*v^2 + alpha*(1-u)
    dv/dt = D_v * grad^2(v) + u*v^2 - (alpha+beta)*v

qizhong u shi huojinongdu, v shi yizhiwu, D_u he D_v shi kuisan
xishu, alpha, beta shi fanying sudu. Tiaozhe zhexie canshu keyi
chansheng bandian, tiwen he jiedu, moni zhenhuaban de huawen.

Huaban zhong de fenshi fencha zunxun zixiangsi guize. Mei ci
diehua shi, huaban pian duan cheng gu dingjiao fenlie cheng geng
xiao de pian duan, xingcheng jingxi de ye mai huawen. Zhenhuaban
bianyuan de Hausdorff weidu tongchang zai 1.2 dao 1.6 zhijian,
biaoming zhongdeng fenshi fuzadu.


4. Zuhe jishu
--------------
Yishu xing zhiwu zuhe zunxun shuxue he chuantong yishu de chengli
yuanli.

Huashu: Duo ge FlowerCurves shili yong CompositionCurves.bouquet()
fenbu. Suanfa jiang huahui an Fibonacci luoxuan fangzhi zai yuanxing
bianjie nei, peihe qingwei de suiji pianyi yi zengqiang ziran gan.

Huayuan changjing: PlantCurves zai butong shendu shengcheng zhi ye,
FlowerCurves tianjia huaduo. Huayuan buzhi yong Voronoi fenkuai fa
fenpei shengzhang quyu, quebao ziran de jianju.

Huaquan: Yuanke junyun fenbu zai yuanxing luoxian shang, yong huchang
canshu hua. Mei ge yuanke chuiyu yuan zai gai dian de qiexian,
xingsheng lianxu de zhiwu huaquan.

Ikebana: Riben huahua yishu qiangdiao budui chen he liukong. PolyArt
de ikebana fangfa jiang san gen zhuyao jing an zhiyuan (shi), di (chi)
he ren (jin) de jiao fangzhi, zunxun gudian Sogetsu xuexiao de
bili.

Cengci shenjdu tongguo toushi suofang he kongzhi Zxu paixu shixian,
shizuhe juyou titigan.


5. Yingyong
-----------
Duoxiangshi huahui moshi you guangfan de shijian yingyong.

Youxi chengxu zhuanyuan ziwu: shishi shengcheng kaifang shijie youxi
zhong de zhiwu. Canshuhua fangfa yunxu wuxian bianhua, neicun xiaohao
jidi, yinwei zhi xuyao cunchu quxian canshu.

Shengchengshi yishu: Yishujia shiyong PolyArt chuangzao banhua,
donghua he hudong shi zhanpin. Shuxue jichu quebao mei jian zuopin
meiyan qie jingque.

Shuxue jiaoyu: Huahui duoxiangshi moshi zuowei yuansuanhe he
jiehe jixue kecheng de xingqu anli. Xueshengtongguo dongshou goujian
quxian tanjiu jizuo zuobiao, canshu fangcheng he duicheng.

Kexue kejihua: Zhiwuxuejia yong quxian moni huahui xingtai, yong
yu fenleixue jianbie. Canshu biaoda keyi dui butong wuzhong de
huaban xingtai jinxing dingliang bijiao.

Fangzhi he biaomian sheji: Huahui duoxiangshi quxian yongyu zhipin,
bihua he taoci zhuangshi, zhe li zhi de zhi de shi keyong de
jixu mei.


Jielun
------
Shuxue shi mei de putong yuyan. Cong jiandan de meiguiquxian dao
xiangrikuizhong de fuza yexu xulie, duoxiangshi quxian jingzhun de
boduo zhiwu xingtai de benzhi, yu ziran bimeiyi. PolyArt moni
kuangjia zhengming, suanfa siwei zengqiang le ery fei xiaoshi
yishu biaoda. Sui zhe women dui ziran xingtai beihou shuxue
jiegou renshi de jiashen, women budan huo de jishu gongju, geng
huide zhen qie de xinshang meige huaban, meipian ye he meigen
jing zhong bencode jingmei.
